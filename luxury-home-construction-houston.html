Luxury is not simply about square footage or price per foot. It is about the integrity of every joint, the weight of every door handle, and the seamless way a home responds to the way you live. At Solanji Construction, we build residences that embody that standard in every detail.

Under the direct oversight of owner Mohamed Solanji, each luxury project begins with a deep understanding of the client's expectations and ends with a home that surpasses them. We source materials from the world's leading suppliers — Italian marble, quarter-sawn hardwoods, custom-forged metalwork, and architectural stone — and install them with the precision they demand. Every finish is hand-selected, every system engineered for performance, and every trade held to the same exacting standard.

From grand estates in River Oaks to contemporary showpieces in Memorial, our luxury builds reflect decades of experience in Houston's most discerning neighborhoods. We understand the architectural traditions, deed restrictions, and construction challenges unique to each community — and we deliver homes that stand apart.

Common Questions

Luxury Home Construction FAQ

How much does luxury home construction cost in Houston?
Luxury home construction in Houston generally starts around $350 per square foot and can exceed $600 or more, depending on architectural complexity, material selections, and site conditions. Homes featuring imported stone, custom metalwork, and high-end automation systems will fall toward the upper end of that range. We provide fully itemized estimates so there are no surprises. For more detail, visit our cost guide.
What is the typical timeline for a luxury home build?
Most luxury residences require 14 to 24 months of construction time, with larger or more architecturally complex homes occasionally extending beyond that. The design and pre-construction planning phase typically adds an additional 3 to 6 months. We establish a detailed milestone schedule during planning and provide regular progress updates throughout the build.
What premium materials do you use in luxury builds?
Our material palette includes natural quartzite and marble slabs, solid hardwood flooring, custom-milled cabinetry, hand-forged iron elements, imported porcelain and ceramic tile, and commercial-grade window and door systems. Every material is evaluated for aesthetic quality, structural performance, and suitability to Houston's subtropical climate.
Do you coordinate with architects and interior designers?
Absolutely. Collaboration with architects and designers is central to our process. We engage early in the design phase to provide constructability input, value-engineering recommendations, and material sourcing guidance — ensuring the design vision translates faithfully into the finished home without budget surprises or compromises.
Are luxury homes in Houston energy efficient?
They can and should be. We integrate high-performance insulation, zoned HVAC systems, Low-E glass, and energy-efficient appliances into every luxury build. Many of our clients also incorporate solar readiness, smart home energy management, and advanced air sealing techniques. A well-built luxury home in Houston should deliver superior comfort while minimizing energy waste.
Does a luxury custom home hold its resale value?
In Houston's premier neighborhoods — including River Oaks, Memorial, and Tanglewood — well-built luxury homes consistently hold and appreciate in value. Quality construction, premium materials, and thoughtful design are the primary drivers. Homes built with inferior materials or poor craftsmanship, regardless of price, do not perform the same way in the market.

Common Questions

General Contracting FAQ

What does a general contractor do?
A general contractor serves as the central manager of a construction project. We coordinate all subcontractors and trades, procure materials, manage the project schedule, ensure building code compliance, handle permitting and inspections, control the budget, and serve as the client's single point of contact throughout the entire process. In short, we take full responsibility for delivering your project on time and to standard.
How do I choose the right general contractor in Houston?
Look for a licensed and insured contractor with a verifiable track record in your type of project. Ask for references and visit completed work if possible. Ensure they provide detailed written estimates, clear contracts, and direct access to the person managing your build. Avoid contractors who are difficult to reach, vague about pricing, or unwilling to provide proof of licensing and insurance.
How much does it cost to hire a general contractor?
General contractor fees typically range from 10% to 20% of total project costs, depending on complexity, scope, and the level of service provided. At Solanji Construction, our fees are transparent and included in your project estimate. There are no hidden markups. For detailed cost information on residential builds, visit our cost guide.
How do you manage project timelines?
We create a detailed milestone schedule during pre-construction that maps every phase of the project — from permitting through final walkthrough. This schedule is shared with you and updated regularly. Mohamed's daily on-site presence allows us to identify and resolve potential delays before they impact the overall timeline, keeping your project moving forward efficiently.
What permits are required for residential construction in Houston?
The City of Houston requires building permits for new construction, structural modifications, and most work involving plumbing, electrical, or mechanical systems. Depending on the project, additional permits may be required for demolition, driveway cuts, tree removal, or work within floodplain areas. We manage every permit application and schedule all required inspections on your behalf.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Solanji Construction is fully licensed and insured for residential construction in the State of Texas and the City of Houston. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We are happy to provide proof of insurance and licensing documentation upon request — and we recommend that you require the same of any contractor you consider.

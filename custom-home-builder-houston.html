Common Questions

Custom Home Building FAQ

How long does it take to build a custom home in Houston?
Most custom homes take between 10 and 18 months to complete, depending on size, complexity, and design features. We provide a detailed milestone schedule during the planning phase so you always know what to expect. Factors like permitting timelines, weather, and material lead times can influence the overall duration.
How much does it cost to build a custom home in Houston?
Custom home construction in Houston typically ranges from $200 to $500+ per square foot, depending on finishes, lot conditions, and architectural complexity. We provide transparent, itemized estimates before construction begins. For a detailed breakdown, visit our cost to build a home in Houston guide.
Do I need to have an architect before hiring a builder?
Not necessarily. While some clients come to us with completed architectural plans, others start with just a vision. We can recommend architects and designers we trust and collaborate closely with them throughout the design process to ensure buildability and budget alignment.
What neighborhoods in Houston do you build in?
We build custom homes throughout Greater Houston, including River Oaks, Memorial, West University, Tanglewood, Bellaire, the Heights, and surrounding communities. If you have a lot in mind, we'd be happy to evaluate it.
Will I have direct access to the owner during the build?
Absolutely. Mohamed Solanji is personally involved in every project from start to finish. You'll have his direct contact information and regular progress updates. This level of owner-led accountability is central to how we operate.
Can you build on my existing lot or do you sell lots?
We build on client-owned lots throughout Houston. We do not sell lots, but we can help evaluate potential properties for buildability, utility access, drainage, and any deed restrictions that may affect your design.

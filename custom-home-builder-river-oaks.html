Common Questions

Building in River Oaks FAQ

What is the process for building a custom home in River Oaks?
Building in River Oaks involves several additional steps beyond standard Houston construction. After securing a lot, you must work with an architect to develop plans that comply with River Oaks Property Owners deed restrictions. Plans are then submitted to the architectural review committee for approval. Once approved, city permits are obtained and construction can begin. We manage this entire process and ensure every requirement is met efficiently.
What are the deed restrictions in River Oaks?
River Oaks deed restrictions govern building setbacks, lot coverage, building height, architectural style, exterior materials, fencing, and other design elements. These restrictions are enforced by the River Oaks Property Owners association and exist to maintain the neighborhood's character and property values. We are thoroughly familiar with these requirements and factor them into every project from the earliest planning stages.
How does the architectural review committee work?
The River Oaks architectural review committee evaluates proposed construction plans to ensure they comply with deed restrictions and are consistent with the neighborhood's aesthetic standards. Submissions typically include architectural drawings, material specifications, landscape plans, and site plans. The review process can take several weeks, and revisions may be requested. We prepare comprehensive submissions designed to earn approval without unnecessary delays.
How much does it cost to build a custom home in River Oaks?
Custom homes in River Oaks generally start at $400 per square foot and can exceed $700 or more for the highest levels of finish and complexity. Land costs in River Oaks are separate and vary significantly based on lot size and location within the neighborhood. We provide detailed, transparent estimates tailored to your specific design. For broader context, visit our cost to build a home in Houston guide.
Are there lots available in River Oaks?
Available lots in River Oaks are limited and highly sought after. Most new construction involves purchasing an existing property, demolishing the existing structure, and building new. We can help evaluate potential lots for buildability, including assessing tree surveys, utility access, drainage, and the feasibility of your design vision within the lot's constraints.
How long does it take to build a home in River Oaks?
From architectural review approval through completion, most River Oaks custom homes take 14 to 24 months to build, depending on size and complexity. The pre-construction phase — including design, architectural review, and permitting — can add an additional 3 to 6 months. We establish a detailed schedule at the outset and provide regular progress updates throughout the entire process.

The Woodlands, Texas

Building in
Texas's Premier
Master-Planned Community

The Woodlands offers a rare combination of natural beauty, modern infrastructure, and a thriving community — making it one of the most sought-after locations for custom home construction in the Greater Houston area.

With its wooded lots, winding streets, and carefully maintained natural environment, The Woodlands provides an exceptional setting for luxury custom homes. The community's architectural guidelines and development standards ensure that every new home contributes to the area's established character and long-term value.

Solanji Construction brings decades of residential construction experience to The Woodlands market. Owner Mohamed Solanji oversees every project personally — from navigating community architectural review processes to coordinating with local suppliers and trade partners who understand the specific requirements of building in this area.

Considering a custom build in The Woodlands? Explore our Houston construction cost guide or contact us to discuss your project.

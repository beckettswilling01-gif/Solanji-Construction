Houston Custom Homes

Your Vision,
Built Without
Compromise

Building a custom home is one of the most significant investments you will ever make. At Solanji Construction, we treat it that way — with the care, precision, and personal attention that a project of this magnitude deserves.

Led by owner Mohamed Solanji, our team has been building custom residences across Greater Houston for decades. We manage every phase of the construction process — from initial site evaluation and architectural coordination through framing, mechanical systems, interior finishes, and final walkthrough. Every detail is executed to exacting standards because we believe your home should reflect nothing less than the best.

Whether you are envisioning a contemporary estate in River Oaks, a transitional home in Memorial, or a luxury residence in The Woodlands, we bring the same level of craftsmanship and accountability to every build. Our approach is simple: listen carefully, plan thoroughly, build meticulously, and deliver a home that exceeds expectations.

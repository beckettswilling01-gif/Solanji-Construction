Building in
Houston's Premier
Family Community

The Memorial area — including Memorial Villages, Tanglewood, and the surrounding neighborhoods — is one of Houston's most desirable addresses for families seeking space, privacy, and proximity to the city's best schools and amenities.

Building a custom home in Memorial requires careful attention to the area's deed restrictions, which often specify minimum lot coverage ratios, setback requirements, and exterior material standards. The established tree canopy, generous lot sizes, and quiet residential streets create a setting where quality construction is not just expected — it is essential.

Solanji Construction has deep experience building custom residences in the Memorial area. Owner Mohamed Solanji personally oversees every project, ensuring that each home is built to the exacting standards these communities demand — from foundation engineering suited to the area's soil conditions to premium finishes that reflect the neighborhood's character.

Considering a custom build in Memorial? Explore our Houston construction cost guide or contact us to discuss your project.

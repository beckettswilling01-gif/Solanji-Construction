Building in
Houston's Most
Prestigious Address

River Oaks is synonymous with luxury living in Houston. With its tree-lined boulevards, expansive lots, and some of the most architecturally significant residences in the state, this neighborhood sets the standard for custom home construction.

Building a custom home in River Oaks requires a builder who understands the unique demands of this community — from navigating architectural review boards and deed restrictions to selecting materials and finishes that meet the elevated expectations of the neighborhood. Every detail, from exterior masonry to interior millwork, is scrutinized here.

Solanji Construction has been building custom residences in and around River Oaks for decades. Led by owner Mohamed Solanji, our team brings hands-on expertise to every phase of the construction process — ensuring your home reflects both the prestige of the neighborhood and your personal vision.

Considering a custom build in River Oaks? Explore our Houston construction cost guide or contact us to discuss your project.
